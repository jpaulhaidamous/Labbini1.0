Usage Guidelines

Brand Do's & Don'ts

Keep the brand consistent across all touchpoints.

Do

Always

  • Use the official logo files provided
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use brand colors from the palette
  • Keep Arabic text right-aligned (RTL)
  • Use Cairo font for Arabic content
  • Ensure sufficient color contrast
Don't

Never

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add effects or shadows to the logo
  • Use non-brand colors in primary UI
  • Use English fonts for Arabic text
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
